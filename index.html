  • implemented online attendance management system

    1.Accuracy Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computational devices. Automatic attendance management systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable and costly errors with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performance and payroll data.
    2. Economy Have you ever thought that attendance management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to inaccurate time reporting, buddy punching, absenteeis, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment. As a matter of fact, a study done by the Axsium Groups found that large organizations saves up to $1600 per employee after implementing Automatic attendance management systems
